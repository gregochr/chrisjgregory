Liminal

Smart Wake & Sleep Music Automation

A scheduled wake-up and sleep application that orchestrates music on smart speakers and lighting via Philips Hue to create research-backed transitions into and out of sleep. Volume fades follow psychoacoustic curves — logarithmic decay for sleep, exponential ramp for waking — while Hue brightness follows a cubic curve to match how the eye perceives light, shifting colour temperature from warm red to daylight to simulate dawn and dusk. Light leads sound by 20 minutes at wake-up, working through closed eyelids to ease sleep stage before music begins. Built to support people who might need gentle transitions into and out of sleep to support their mental health — reliability and zero friction are essential. Supports Sonos multi-room grouping and Spotify Connect, with per-context service selection so wake and sleep can use independent music sources. Includes an escalating snooze cycle that holds light and nudges volume rather than restarting the dawn, and power nap presets with compressed durations where lights dim to help you drop off and gently restore when it's time to wake.
